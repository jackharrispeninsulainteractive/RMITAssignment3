Group Processes

For A2 overall we worked extremely well as a group, we have realistically submitted feedback to each other and are committed to working with all the group members to ensuring that this team functions correctly and efficiently.

From examining our final group reflection, we have really identified what works for us and what needs improvement and moving into A3 & A5 we are really going focused in on those identified areas. These include:


Group Process Focused Improvements for A3 & A5
  1. MS Teams Usage & communication (Written)
  2. Even time management & making a proactive start on assignment content.
  3. Submitting key drafts to the group for review prior to the assignmentâ€™s submission.
  4. Increased group communication (Written mid-week between meetings).

Current Group Process That Works Extremely Well!
  1. Trello boards for managing the projects.
  2. MS Team meetings (Verbal / Video)
  3. Team cohesion
  4. No Personality clashes
  5. Joint goals and a commitment to work as one unit.

What Changes are we going to implement for A3 & A5?

From examining these lists, we will look at changing how we use nonverbal communication and really make a focused effort to work together as a group! We are incredible with video chats, but we fall short with written messaging on MS Teams our biggest change will be a focused push to improve all verbal communication between the group. We will also really be focused on submitting the content as we complete it in draft form, this will allow us to have a greater review process and drastically improve the quality of the work produced by each group members. Finally, the last key point is on time management, introducing the drafting requirements will help elevate this as the main issue revolved around receiving the documents at the 11th hour for compilation to the main assignment, having the early draft review resolves that group issue as everything must be submitted early.
