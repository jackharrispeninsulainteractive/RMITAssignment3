Summary

From the above table we can see that all the team members have some fantastic strengths and weakness to be aware of whilst heading into this group assignment. Jack has a fantastic intellect and drive, whilst also having an overconfident and over critical mind. Noah is fantastically creative and passionate but is sensitive to criticism and prone to burn out. Jaydyn is skilled at problem solving and teamwork but is very unagreeable. And lastly Alex is idealistic and passionate whilst also being self-isolating and self-critical.


During the first few days of the group, it’ll be key for us as a team to really focus on understanding each other’s strengths and weaknesses and really focusing in on accommodating them and working with each other provide the best working environment and the best situation to allow each of us as a individual to thrive and give 110%.
