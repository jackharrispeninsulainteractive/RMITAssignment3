Group Reflection

Assignment Credit: Jack Harris (Overview + Reflection), Noah Jacobson, Jaydyn Hoskings, Alex Vorraro
Overview

Examining the core elements of the reviews from our team we can see a number of trends; these mainly focus on the improvements on the teamwork. Everyone has really highlighted this as a key area that the team has performed extremely well in. Looking at individual elements and members of the group we can see that Jaydyn has really stepped up this assignment and increase his interactivity with team by showing initiative and a want and drive to get into teaming and work together the project. Noah has really come into his element whilst working on our logos, design and branding, we can see that this is really a passion of his and its so fantastic to see that be applied to the group and project. Alex has really stepped up into the group work and been present and active at way more meeting than A2! He has also done fantastic work on our project aims & group communication sections. Finally Jack has done a great job loading the PHP development of our app, its so fantastic and exciting to see how much progress has been made in such short time! To summarize as a group we have worked even better than A2 and if we would continue working together we would only be expecting even better and greater things!
