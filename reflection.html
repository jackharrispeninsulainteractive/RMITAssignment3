Group Reflection

Assignment Credit: Jack Harris (Overview + Reflection), Noah Jacobson, Jaydyn Hoskings, Alex Vorraro
Overview

All the group members have submitted some great feedback for this group assessment, this overview will summaries our feedback as one team. Overall, all the group members have reported that we have functioned extremely well. Some great key feedback has been that Trello has been extremely well received for project management, Jack has used the software before, but it was new to Jaydyn, Alex and Noah, both Alex and Noah have commented on how effective it was at keeping us on track and monitoring exactly what tasks are in progress and what tasks are completed. On the counter to this the universal feedback was that MS Teams did not suit the teamwork flow, all the team members made every effort to use it and will continue to for A3 & A5 but the reflection has been that it is a worse method of communication than Discord & Slack.

(NOTE: This is a personal preference for our groups workflow, MS Teams may suit a different group, we also understand this is a requirement and not debatable and will ensure its used for the duration of the assessments.)

Alex and Jack have provided some great feedback about time management and communication, Alex has suggested we focus quicker on the assignment and jump start into it sooner, Jack and suggested we focus on real time updates of where we are all at on Trello, these two suggestions will be incorporated into the group for A3 & A5 and really build upon the current fantastic team cohesion that we currently have.

Noah and Alex and complimented Jack’s ability to step in as project lead for the team, looking back at our personality tests this aligns correctly with what was stated in the personality profile and team analysis conducted during this assignment. Expanding on that Jack has provided great feedback for Noah, Alex and Jaydyn, complimenting them on their dedicated, creativity and input to the team, this sentiment was reflected across all the team members reviews.

To summaries our reflections indicate the success of the team to function cohesively, each group member has contributed and reflected their positive thoughts about the other group members during each of the reflections. We have identified some great areas for improvement and are all exited to jump into A3 & A5!
