As we formed the group for this assignment it was pretty daunting as I haven't done that many group assignments throughout my studies but as we got into doing the meetings I found that we seem to work well together, With Jack being a really good group leader laying at each of the tasks into short step but step easy to follow objectives this allowed us to relax as we had a clear goal for each of the modules allocated to each of us.
