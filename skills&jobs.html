Jack Harris

Jack’s skills and strengths lie in leadership and previous knowledge due to his background in website development and programming and previous uses of Technologies and tools we used when developing our Project/Website. Jack had already shown knowledge of group management due to his background as a Lifeguard Supervisor and took a leading role within the group. With his extensive knowledge of coding and website development Jack took the job of Backend developer for the website and provided support to the rest of the group such as giving support to Noah the front end developer for the website and allocating work for the group. Jack had previously worked with tools such as phpstorm and Cms (Wordpress) and implemented these skills into the assignment using them for the development of the website.


Noah Jacobson

Noah showed great use of his skills during this assignment taking on the role of creating the video for the presentation part of the assignment, due to his background in video editing, Noah also took the job of being the Front-end developer and demonstrated his skills in UX design and also stepped up to the role of designing our logo used for our business, though he used a program he is not familiar with he did have previous experience working with photoshop a similar program to what he used for the logo.
